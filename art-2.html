Evrything Is Motion

Installation made by Cornelia Parker where she made it from a stack of woods that are collected from the remains of exploded warehouse. It reminds Cornelia of her and other childrens childhood where they use to play with the things that are placed in the attic or warehouse after they grew up. It reminds her of the memory of childhood where she also used to play with her friends.
