Spoliarium
An Exploded View

An abstract art made by Jaison Cianelli that attracts our eyes with warming colors. Light and shadows that create waves and made it seem real like we are enjoying a sunset. Somehow the texture of the art that Jaison painted make the waves looks like its moving forward and backward.

Jaison wanted to shatre her love for nature by expressing her feelings into the painting and also spotlighting some aspect to the art. She was also inspired by a beautiful sunset that she wanted to share what Jaison ejoys the wonderful effect to people.
