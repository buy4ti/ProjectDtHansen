A short thriller story that tell us about the main character, Cadence, who is struggling with memory loss because of her head injury. Her mother sent her on a trip with her father in Europe, although she usually spends every summer with all her cousins on an island. Cadence had found that things in the family has changed a lot, but no one answers on what is actually happening. The story can not be solved easily until the twist comes, the story also comes with lies and secrets that are shocking.
